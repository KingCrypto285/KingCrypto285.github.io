Topic-description

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence and allows us to simulate human intelligence in machines.
This involves creating and generating algorithms to sort and calculate through large amounts of data.

With the introduction of AI, we find the technology doing tasks for us such as:

  • Manufacturing robots.
  • Self-driving cars.
  • Smart assistants.
  • Proactive healthcare management.

    We chose AI because it looks very relevant to present and upcoming technologies.
    AI has become extremely useful as a tool with the huge amounts of data there is to store, manage and extract.
    We want to look into how it can be misused in everyday situations.
